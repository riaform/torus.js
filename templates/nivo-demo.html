Use the Kockout custom binding nivoSlider in conjunction with a foreach loop over images. All properties in the binding are passed into nivo control as settings.

    <div class="list frontSlide nivoSlider" data-bind="foreach: urls, nivoSlider: { effect:'random', controlNav:false, directionNav:false }">
        <img data-bind="attr: { src: $data }" />
    </div>
